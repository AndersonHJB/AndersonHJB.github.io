1. Suppose that $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \text{Exponential}(\lambda)$ , so that the log-likelihood is

$$
\ell(\lambda; x) = \log f_n(x; \lambda) = c + n \log \lambda - \lambda \bar{x}
$$

where $\bar{x} := n^{-1} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i$ , and c is some constant. This is a differentiable function of λ,and so to find the Maximum Likelihood estimate (ML estimate), we can differentiate and then solve the first-order conditions. Differentiating gives
$$
\ell'(X; \lambda) = \frac{n}{\lambda} - n\bar{x}
$$
Setting this equal to zero and solving for λ gives
$$
\frac{n}{\lambda} - n\bar{x} = 0 \implies \lambda = \frac{1}{\bar{x}}.
$$
Note that $\ell''(\lambda; X) = -\frac{n}{\lambda^2} < 0 \text{ for all } \lambda$ , so this value maximizes $\ell$ . Hence the ML estimate is 
$$
\hat{\lambda}(X) = \frac{1}{\bar{x}}
$$
Now derive the ML estimates for the following distributions in a similar manner. Note that the final distribution must be handled differently, as you will see that $f(x; a, b)$ is not a smooth function of a or b.

(a)$X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \ \text{Poisson}(\lambda)$

(b) $X \sim \text{Binomial}(n, p)$ , where n is specified (i.e. only p is unknown).

(c) $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \ \text{Normal}(\mu, \sigma^2)$

(d) $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \ \text{Uniform}(a, b)$

2. In this question and the next we will use the Pareto distribution, whose PDF may be written

$$
f_X(x; x_0, \theta) = \frac{\theta x_0^\theta}{x^{\theta + 1}} \mathbb{I}(x \ge x_0),
$$

$\text{where } x_0 > 0 \text{ and } \theta > 0.$

(a)  Show that the CDF, or distribution function, of $X$ is
$$
F_X(x; x_0, \theta) = \begin{cases} 
1 - \left(\frac{x_0}{x}\right)^\theta \mathbb{I} (x \geq x_0)
\end{cases}
$$
(b) Show that the quantile function of $X$ is
$$
F^{-1}_X(u; x_0, \theta) = x_0(1 - u)^{-1/\theta}
$$
$\text{for} \space 0 < u < 1.$

[**Hint:** the quantile function is defined as follows: for any ( $u \in (0,1)$ ) one wants to find the smallest ( $x \in \mathbb{R}$ ) such that

$$F_X(x) \geq u,$$

that is more formally,

$$x = F_X^{-}(u) := \inf\{y \in \mathbb{R} : F_X(y) \geq u\}.$$

$F_X^{-}$ is a generalized inverse, as in general $F_x$ need not be continuous and strictly increasing. In the present case you can find $F_X^{-1}$ , the actual inverse of $F_x$ , e.g. by finding $x \in \mathbb{R}$  such that $Fx(x) = u$ for an arbitrary $u \in (0,1)$].

(c) How can we generate random quantities from this distribution?

[ $\text{Hint:} \text{ show that the CDF of the r.v. } Y = F_X^{-1}(U) \text{ for } U \sim \text{Uniform}(0, 1) \text{ is the same as } X \sim \text{Pareto}(x_0, \theta)$ ]

3. In R we can write functions ppareto, dpareto, qpareto, and rpareto, to compute the probability function of x, the density function of x, the quantile function of u, and n random realizations of X.

## 1. 导出以下分布的最大似然估计（ML estimates）

### (a) $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \text{Poisson}(\lambda)$

给定 Poisson 分布的概率密度函数：
$$
f(x;\lambda) = \frac{e^{-\lambda} \lambda^x}{x!}
$$
似然函数为：
$$
L(\lambda;x) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i;\lambda) = e^{-n\lambda} \lambda^{\sum x_i} / \prod_{i=1}^{n} x_i!
$$
对数似然函数为：
$$
\ell(\lambda;x) = -n\lambda + \log(\lambda) \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i - \log \prod_{i=1}^{n} x_i!
$$
求导并令其为0，得到：
$$
\frac{d\ell}{d\lambda} = -n + \frac{\sum x_i}{\lambda} = 0
$$
从中我们可以解得：
$$
\lambda = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i}{n} = \bar{x}
$$

### (b) $X \sim \text{Binomial}(n, p)$

给定二项分布的概率密度函数：
$$
f(x;n,p) = \binom{n}{x} p^x (1-p)^{n-x}
$$
对数似然函数为：
$$
\ell(p;x) = \log \binom{n}{x_i} + x_i \log p + (n-x_i) \log(1-p)
$$
求导并令其为 0，得到：
$$
\frac{d\ell}{dp} = \frac{x_i}{p} - \frac{n-x_i}{1-p} = 0
$$
解得：
$$
p = \frac{x_i}{n}
$$

### (c) $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \text{Normal}(\mu, \sigma^2)$

对于正态分布，其概率密度函数为：
$$
f(x;\mu, \sigma^2) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}}e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$
对数似然函数为：
$$
\ell(\mu, \sigma^2;x) = -\frac{n}{2}\log(2\pi\sigma^2) - \frac{1}{2\sigma^2}\sum_{i=1}^n (x_i-\mu)^2
$$


对于正态分布，最大似然估计为：
$$
\mu = \bar{x}
$$
和
$$
\sigma^2 = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \bar{x})^2
$$

### (d) $X \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \text{Uniform}(a, b)$

对于均匀分布，最大似然估计为：
$$
a = \min(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n)
$$
和
$$
b = \max(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n)
$$

## 2. Pareto 分布

### (a) 显示 CDF

从PDF开始，我们可以得到CDF：
$$
F_X(x; x_0, \theta) = \int_{x_0}^{x} f_X(t; x_0, \theta) dt = \int_{x_0}^{x} \frac{\theta x_0^\theta}{t^{\theta + 1}} dt = 1 - \left(\frac{x_0}{x}\right)^\theta \mathbb{I} (x \geq x_0)
$$

### (b) 显示分位数函数

根据提示，我们有：
$$
F_X(x) = u
$$
插入 CDF，并解出 x，得到：
$$
x = F_X^{-1}(u; x_0, \theta) = x_0(1 - u)^{-1/\theta}
$$

### (c) 生成随机数

根据提示，我们有：
$$
Y = F_X^{-1}(U)
$$
其中( $U \sim \text{Uniform}(0, 1)$ )。

这意味着可以从均匀分布中取样，并通过 Pareto 的逆 CDF 将其转换为 Pareto 分布的随机样本。
