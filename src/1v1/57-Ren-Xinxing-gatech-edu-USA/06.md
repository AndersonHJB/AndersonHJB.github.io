Let $U$ be a $\text{Unif}(0,1)$ random variable. Which of the following functions of $U$ will give you a realization of $X$?

- (a) $X = U^{1/3}$
- (b) $X = U^{1/2}$
- (c) $X = (1/3)U^{1/3}$
- (d) $X = (1/3)U^{1/2}$
- (e) $X = 3U^2$
